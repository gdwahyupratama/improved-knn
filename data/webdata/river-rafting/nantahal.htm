Nantahala River Rafting

 

The Nantahala River offers the thrill and excitement of riding the river rapids in a raft, canoe or kayak. Eight miles of waves and swift, clear currents make the Nantahala River a delight for almost all ages interested in learning whitewater rafting. The trip takes a little over 3 hours and contains several class I and II rapids, with an optional class III at the end.  No matter if you go-it alone or with a guide, it will be a memory you'll treasure for a lifetime. Please note that in my extensive research I have found that no one under the weight of 60 lbs. is allowed to ride down the Nantahala River. Also, the above photo is a picture of rafters taking the Wesser Falls during competition. These falls are not open to the public for rafting without a guide and special arrangements.
