There is no experience in the world that measures up to the grandeur and history of the Grand Canyon. Major John Wesley Powell and ten brave men were the first to discover the magic and beauty of the Canyon during their historic descent in 1869. Since that trip, thousands of people from all walks of life have explored the many side canyons, waterfalls, wildlife and have tried to tame the thundering rapids of the Colorado.

Arizona River Runners offers complete package trips that include your river equipment, delightful meals, river interpretations, folklore and optional transportation.
