River Ratings - What They Mean
Class I Relaxing - gentle floating
Class II Easy Does It - moderate waves, clear passages
Class III Adventurous - waterslide-like waves, clear passages
Class IV Thrilling - powerful waves, big drops
Class V Awesome - big waves, technical drops and steep grade
