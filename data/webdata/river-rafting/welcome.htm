Welcome to C.R.O. A.
When it comes to selecting a river rafting adventure - the Colorado River Outfitter's (CROA) more than 50 licensed river outfitters are professionally trained and experienced in navigating Colorado's 13 river systems. In addition to river running many outfitters also offer combination packages which include many outdoor recreation options, including fishing, whitewater kayaking, hiking, mountain biking, camping, horseback riding, photography and much more!
