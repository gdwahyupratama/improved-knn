What's New
Toronto, York pledge to work together on regional transit initiatives
The Globe and Mail (3/26)
In a rare show of unity, politicians from the City of Toronto and York Region pledged yesterday to co-operate on new cross-boundary transit projects that, over time, would offer commuters an alternative to their cars.
