PRODUCTIVE - What sets us apart

Waterloo is one of Canada's leading universities, with undergraduate and graduate programs in applied health sciences, arts, engineering, environmental studies, mathematics, and science. We're also research-intensive-committed to discovering new knowledge and finding ways to use that knowledge. In addition, we are home to the largest co-operative education program in the world.

CHOICE -The right student for you

No other university or college in Canada comes close to offering you the selection you get from Waterloo. From applied studies to computer science, biology to environmental engineering, our students span a comprehensive range of over 100 disciplines at the undergraduate and graduate levels.
