7.7 Strikes

Occasionally, co-op students are involved in a labour disruption. Follow these guidelines:

  • In any instance where a strike or labour disruption is anticipated, discuss the situation with your employer and your field co-ordinator. Ask your employer about crossing the picket line. In many cases alternate arrangements for work can be made.

  • The length and timing of the strike determines if credit for the work term is allowed.

  • If your work term is jeopardized by a prolonged strike and you choose to leave the job, CECS will make every effort to find you another job. However, CECS does not guarantee substitute employment.

  • Payment of your wages during a labour disruption is the decision of the employer.

Maintain constant communication with your field co-ordinator during a labour disruption. CECS makes every effort to monitor the situation.
