10.1 Salary

The employer determines the salary paid to the co-op student. Salary varies based upon your program of study and work term level, but usually increases as you become more experienced.

CECS prepares a list of salary averages and ranges according to academic program and work term. This list is available to employers and serves as a guideline for remuneration. You may view the list online or on the bulletin board in Needles Hall. Keep in mind that the list is a guideline only. CECS can recommend, but not dictate, salary levels to employers.

Salary is a consideration in choosing a job, but it should not be the only factor. Look for a position that provides you with the most valuable experience.

Employers are not required to pay overtime.
