10.2 Income Tax

At the beginning of your work term you may be expected to complete a TD1 form for tax purposes. You do not need to pay taxes if your income is below the exemption limit; otherwise you are expected to pay taxes. Your employer can provide a more detailed explanation of paying income tax when you begin your work term.

For more information about income tax, contact the nearest Canada Customs and Revenue Agency office and request a copy of Students and Income Tax. This document is also available on the web. For general income tax inquiries, you may also call the Agency at 1-800-959-8281.
