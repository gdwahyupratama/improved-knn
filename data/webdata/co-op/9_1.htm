Applied Health Sciences

  • Health Studies

  • Kinesiology

  • Recreation

Hand in your work report to your department.

Your work report is marked by:

  • a grad student for the 1st and 2nd reports

  • a faculty member for the 3rd and 4th reports

  • a faculty member or field co-ordinator for confidential reports

A secondary evaluation by your employer is strongly recommended, but not mandatory

 

Use the Applied Health Sciences report guidelines instead of the ones in this chapter. Follow the link on the title "Applied Health Sciences" above.
