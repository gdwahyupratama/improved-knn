9.7 Confidential Reports

Your employer may insist that a report not be submitted because of the confidential nature of the information contained in it. If your report is confidential, inform your field co-ordinator. Provisions may be made for the employer to evaluate a work report if it is confidential. You must obtain prior permission from your field co-ordinator or faculty to ensure that you receive credit for the work report. Confidential reports are not normally marked until the following term.

 

Some faculties, departments or employers do not accept confidential work reports. See the Faculty of Engineering Supplement to Work Report Writing Guidelines for more information.

Work reports at IBM Canada Ltd. must be non-confidential. Work reports assessed as confidential by IBM will not be evaluated for credit.
