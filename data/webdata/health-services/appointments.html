Scheduling an Appointment

Appointments are often available on the same day. You can either drop in the health services building or call 888-4096 for the first available appointment.

*Please allow 24 hours for cancelling an appointment. Otherwise there will be a charge for each appointment missed. Cancel on our 24 hour, 7 day a week voice prompt at 888-4096.
