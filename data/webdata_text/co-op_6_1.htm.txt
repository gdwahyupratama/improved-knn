6.1 about learning objectives 6.1 about learning objectives learning objectives motivate students to maximize the benefits of a work term. be sure to set achievable and measurable goals so you can: get more out of your job experience develop life skills focus your career goals. learning objectives are an important part of a successful work term and should be discussed with your field co-ordinator. work terms provide excellent opportunities to continue learning; accepting and merely performing the job is unfortunate. relate what you have learned in class to your work and determine if it fits with your career objectives. to motivate learning while on work terms, set goals. using learning objectives enhances your life skills and gives you greater responsibility for your education. writing your objectives provides a reminder of what you wish to accomplish. you benefit from the learning objectives concept, but remember that your results reflect the effort you expend.
