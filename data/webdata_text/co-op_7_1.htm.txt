7.8 co-op graduation requirements 7.1 co-op graduation requirements 7.1.1 quantity the minimum number of work terms required for graduation is four for students in arts, applied health sciences, environmental studies (except architecture), and science and five for students in architecture, engineering and mathematics. you are expected to follow the work term schedule. see appendix a, section b. 7.1.2 academic standards each faculty defines their own academic standards. if you fall below the standards, your faculty may require withdrawal from the co-op program. you can continue in the regular (non co-op) program, unless you are in architecture or engineering. you may be able to re-enter the co-op program if you raise your grades within a specific period of time. check your faculty's academic standards in the university calendar
