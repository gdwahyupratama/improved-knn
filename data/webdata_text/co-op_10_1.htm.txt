10.1 salary 10.1 salary the employer determines the salary paid to the co-op student. salary varies based upon your program of study and work term level, but usually increases as you become more experienced. cecs prepares a list of salary averages and ranges according to academic program and work term. this list is available to employers and serves as a guideline for remuneration. you may view the list online or on the bulletin board in needles hall. keep in mind that the list is a guideline only. cecs can recommend, but not dictate, salary levels to employers. salary is a consideration in choosing a job, but it should not be the only factor. look for a position that provides you with the most valuable experience. employers are not required to pay overtime.
