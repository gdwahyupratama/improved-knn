10.2 income tax 10.2 income tax at the beginning of your work term you may be expected to complete a td1 form for tax purposes. you do not need to pay taxes if your income is below the exemption limit; otherwise you are expected to pay taxes. your employer can provide a more detailed explanation of paying income tax when you begin your work term. for more information about income tax, contact the nearest canada customs and revenue agency office and request a copy of students and income tax. this document is also available on the web. for general income tax inquiries, you may also call the agency at 1-800-959-8281.
