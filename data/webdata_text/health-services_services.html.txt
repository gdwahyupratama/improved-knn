health services - university of waterloo health services health services provides emergency care, health information and nursing services to faculty, staff and students, as well as providing on-campus medical services to students. all students are provided with accessible and confidential health care. urgent care/walk-in-care physicians appointments infirmary day room information nurse (test results - 3:00 pm - 4:30 pm) immunization allergy injections (9:00 am - 4:00 pm) confidential hiv (aids) testing, counselling and health monitoring verification of illness forms (if unable to write mid-terms or final exams due to illness, health services provides the necessary medical assessment and form) nursing services: bp monitoring, on-going post-op treatment, administration of prescribed injectable drugs, health teaching family planning birth control dispensary (9:00 am - 12:00 pm, 2:00 pm - 4:00 pm) morning after pill (urgent contraception) pregnancy tests, (and non-judgmental management of crisis pregnancy) wart treatment (every tuesday) counsellor appointments nutrition counselling individual counselling ear piercing and syringing last update april 03, 2001 this page maintained by chris strome. copyright © university of waterloo
