6.4 application of learning objectives 6.4 application of learning objectives during your work term, discuss your job assignment with your supervisor and review the objectives you have set out. write down main tasks, responsibilities, assignments, supervisory experience, and any other pertinent information. this provides a valuable work record as you proceed from term to term. when preparing résumés, you will be able to include more detail. recognizing a pattern may help you plan your career goals. your work record is also an excellent reference and can aid discussion of your skills during interviews.
